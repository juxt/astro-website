JUXT AI Radar — 2025 Edition

This document presents the JUXT perspective on the evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence technologies, practices, and tools. Inspired by the Thoughtworks Technology Radar, it is structured by quadrants and rings to help you navigate AI adoption strategically.

Techniques
Prompt Engineering
Crafting effective prompts is now a critical skill in AI workflows, particularly with large language models. Prompt libraries and tooling are maturing rapidly.
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
RAG combines LLMs with knowledge retrieval, giving AI access to custom corpora. Effective, but requires careful latency and index tuning.
Platforms
LangChain
A Python/JS framework for chaining LLM calls and tools. Rapidly evolving, but lock-in and abstraction complexity are concerns.
Closed-source LLM Hosting Platforms
While convenient, full reliance on closed platforms limits control, transparency, and cost management. Evaluate OSS/self-hosted alternatives.
